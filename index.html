About OTU

Founded in 2012, Online Tech University (OTU) is Serbia's premier institution for technology education and innovation. Located in Belgrade, our campus combines modern infrastructure with a commitment to digital excellence.

At OTU, we prepare students for success in the rapidly evolving tech landscape through hands-on learning, industry partnerships, and cutting-edge research opportunities.
