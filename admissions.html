Join Our Community of Innovators

At Online Tech University, we're looking for passionate students ready to shape the future of technology.

Our admissions process is designed to identify motivated individuals with potential to excel in our rigorous academic environment. We value diversity of thought, background, and experience, and seek students who will contribute to our vibrant campus community.

Application Process

1. Research Programs

Explore our programs to find the one that aligns with your interests and career goals.

View Programs

2. Prepare Application Materials

  • Completed application form
  • Academic transcripts
  • Personal statement
  • Letters of recommendation
  • Standardized test scores (if applicable)
  • Portfolio (for specific programs)

3. Submit Application

Submit your application and required documents through our online portal by the application deadline.

Application Portal

4. Interview (if required)

Selected candidates may be invited for an interview with faculty or admissions staff, either in person or online.

5. Admission Decision

Receive your admission decision within 4-6 weeks after application deadline.

6. Confirm Enrollment

If accepted, confirm your enrollment and pay the enrollment deposit to secure your place.

Tuition & Financial Aid

Tuition & Fees

Undergraduate Programs

  • EU Students: €3,000 per year
  • Non-EU Students: €5,000 per year

Graduate Programs

  • Master's Programs: €4,500 per year
  • MBA Programs: €7,500 per year
  • PhD Programs: €3,500 per year

Additional Fees

  • Registration Fee: €150 (one-time)
  • Technology Fee: €200 per year
  • Student Services Fee: €100 per year

Financial Aid & Scholarships

Online Tech University is committed to making quality education accessible. We offer various financial aid options:

Merit Scholarships

  • Academic Excellence: Up to 50% tuition reduction
  • Technology Innovation: Up to €3,000 per year
  • Leadership Award: Up to €2,000 per year

Need-Based Aid

  • Online Tech University Grant: Based on financial need assessment
  • Work-Study Program: On-campus employment opportunities

International Student Support

  • Global Diversity Scholarship: Up to 30% tuition reduction
  • Country-Specific Partnership Grants

Frequently Asked Questions

For undergraduate programs, we require a high school diploma or equivalent with a strong academic record, particularly in mathematics and science subjects. For graduate programs, a bachelor's degree in a relevant field with a minimum GPA of 3.0 (or equivalent) is required. Specific programs may have additional requirements.

While we consider SAT/ACT scores as part of our holistic review process, they are not mandatory. Students may apply under our test-optional policy, where we place greater emphasis on academic performance, personal statements, and extracurricular activities.

Yes, we offer conditional admission for international students who meet academic requirements but need to improve their English language proficiency. Students admitted conditionally must complete our Intensive English Program or achieve the required TOEFL/IELTS score before beginning their degree program.

Yes, we accept transfer credits from accredited institutions. Credits must be for courses with a grade of C or higher (or equivalent) and must be applicable to your degree program at Online Tech University. Our transfer credit evaluation team will assess your previous coursework upon admission.

We offer both in-person and virtual campus visits. You can schedule a visit through our online portal, where you can select your preferred date, time, and tour type. Campus visits typically include an information session, tour, and opportunity to meet with faculty and current students.
