Our Location

Directions Guide

By Car

From the city center, drive east along Bulevar kralja Aleksandra for about 5km, then turn right at the Technology Park junction.

Public Transport

Take bus routes 26, 27, or 17 to the "Technology Park" stop, then walk 3 minutes to reach the campus.

From Airport

From Nikola Tesla Airport, take the airport express or a taxi. The journey to campus takes approximately 30 minutes.
