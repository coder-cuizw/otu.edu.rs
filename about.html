Our Mission

At Online Tech University, our mission is to educate and empower the next generation of technology leaders through innovative teaching, cutting-edge research, and meaningful industry partnerships. We are committed to providing a world-class education that prepares students to excel in a rapidly evolving digital landscape.

We believe in fostering a culture of innovation, critical thinking, and ethical responsibility, ensuring our graduates not only possess technical expertise but also the ability to apply their knowledge to solve real-world challenges.

Our Vision

We aspire to be a globally recognized leader in technology education and research, known for our innovative approach to learning and commitment to addressing the digital challenges of the 21st century.

Our vision is to create a dynamic learning environment where students, faculty, and industry partners collaborate to drive technological advancement and create positive societal impact. By 2030, we aim to be among the top technology universities in Europe, with a reputation for excellence, innovation, and inclusivity.

Our History

2012

Foundation

Online Tech University was founded in Belgrade, Serbia by a group of technology experts and educational visionaries who recognized the need for specialized technology education in the region.

2014

First Graduating Class

The university celebrated its first graduating class of 120 students across three pioneering programs: Computer Science, Data Analytics, and Digital Design.

2016

Campus Expansion

Online Tech University expanded its campus with the opening of the Innovation Center, a state-of-the-art facility designed to foster collaboration between students, faculty, and industry partners.

2018

International Recognition

The university received its first international accreditation and established exchange programs with leading technical universities in Europe and North America.

2020

Virtual Learning Platform

In response to global challenges, Online Tech University launched its comprehensive virtual learning platform, setting new standards for online education in the region.

2022

Strategic Vision 2030

Online Tech University celebrated its 10th anniversary with the launch of the Strategic Vision 2030, outlining ambitious goals for research, education, and global impact.

2023

Today

Today, Online Tech University stands as Serbia's leading technology university with over 3,500 students, 250+ faculty members, and partnerships with tech industry leaders across Europe and beyond.

University Leadership

Dr. Milan Jovanović

President

Dr. Jovanović has led Online Tech University since its founding in 2012. With a background in computer science and over 20 years of experience in academia and the tech industry, he brings a unique blend of technical expertise and strategic vision to the university.

Dr. Ana Petrović

Provost

As Provost, Dr. Petrović oversees all academic programs and faculty affairs. Her research in artificial intelligence and commitment to educational innovation have been instrumental in shaping Online Tech University's curriculum.

Prof. Stefan Nikolić

Dean of Research

Prof. Nikolić leads Online Tech University's research initiatives, with a focus on fostering interdisciplinary collaboration and securing partnerships with industry leaders for applied research opportunities.

Our Campus

Modern Facilities in the Heart of Belgrade

Located in Belgrade's vibrant tech district, our campus combines historic architecture with modern amenities designed to support learning, research, and collaboration.

Our facilities include:

  • Innovation Center for startup incubation and industry collaboration
  • Digital Library with access to global research databases
  • Smart classrooms equipped with advanced educational technology
  • Student collaboration spaces designed to foster teamwork and creativity
