Campus Life

Experience a Rich and Diverse University Life

Life at OTU

At Online Tech University of Serbia, we are committed to providing students with a comprehensive campus experience that allows you to enjoy a fulfilling university life while gaining knowledge. Our modern facilities, diverse activities, and welcoming community environment will help you make the most of your university years.

Student Housing

We offer a variety of comfortable and secure housing options to meet the needs and budgets of different students. All residence halls are equipped with high-speed internet, modern facilities, and 24-hour security services.

Tech Park Residence

Located in the center of the main campus, offering single and double rooms with modern facilities and study spaces.

  • High-speed WiFi
  • Private bathrooms
  • Common lounges
  • Shared kitchens

Innovation District Apartments

Suite-style apartments for upperclassmen, providing more private space and independent facilities.

  • High-speed WiFi
  • Private kitchens
  • Living rooms
  • Laundry facilities

Smart Living Apartments

Modern apartments featuring smart home technology, offering students the perfect blend of technology and comfort.

  • Smart control systems
  • Smart climate control
  • Electronic door locks
  • Smart charging stations

Campus Dining

We offer a diverse range of dining options to meet different tastes and dietary needs. From campus dining halls to cafés, from international cuisine to healthy choices, you can enjoy delicious meals right on campus.

Main Tech Dining Hall

Offers a variety of daily meals, including local specialties and international cuisine at affordable prices.

Mon-Fri: 7:00-20:00, Weekend: 8:00-19:00

Innovation Café

Provides quality coffee, tea, and light meals, making it an ideal spot for studying and socializing.

Daily: 7:30-22:00

Digital Fast Food Zone

Offers various fast food options, including burgers, pizza, salads, and international snacks.

Mon-Sun: 10:00-22:00

Healthy Food Corner

Focuses on providing healthy, organic, and vegetarian options to meet special dietary needs.

Mon-Fri: 8:00-19:00
