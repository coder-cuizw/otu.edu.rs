Experience Our Campus

Visit Online Tech University and discover our state-of-the-art facilities, vibrant campus culture, and innovative learning environment. Our campus tours provide prospective students and visitors with an opportunity to explore our modern facilities, meet current students and faculty, and get a feel for life at one of Serbia's leading technology universities.

Whether you're considering applying to our programs or simply interested in learning more about our university, a campus tour is the perfect way to experience Online Tech University firsthand.

Tour Options

Group Tours

Join our scheduled group tours led by current students. Tours run Monday through Friday at 10:00 AM and 2:00 PM, and Saturdays at 11:00 AM. Each tour accommodates up to 15 visitors.

Duration: 90 minutes

Schedule Group Tour

Department Tours

Specialized tours focusing on specific academic departments and their facilities. Meet with faculty members and current students from your program of interest.

Duration: 2 hours

Schedule Department Tour

Virtual Tours

Can't visit in person? Explore our campus through our interactive virtual tour. Navigate through buildings, classrooms, labs, and outdoor spaces at your own pace.

Available 24/7

Start Virtual Tour

What to Expect on Your Tour

Campus Facilities

Explore our modern academic buildings, Innovation Center, Digital Library, technology labs, and student collaboration spaces.

Technology Showcase

See our cutting-edge equipment, software, and research facilities that support our technology-focused education.

Student Interaction

Meet current students who will share their experiences and answer your questions about life at Online Tech University.

Information Session

Learn about our academic programs, admissions process, financial aid options, and student support services.

Tour Tips

  • Wear comfortable shoes as the tour involves walking throughout campus
  • Arrive 15 minutes before your scheduled tour time
  • Prepare questions for your student guide or faculty members
  • Consider visiting during the academic year to experience campus when classes are in session
  • Free parking is available for campus visitors in designated areas
