Research

Exploring Innovation, Shaping the Future

Research Vision

The Online Tech University of Serbia is dedicated to driving technological innovation and pioneering research, addressing the major challenges facing society today. Our interdisciplinary research teams bring together top talent from around the world to work together toward building a more intelligent and sustainable future.

25+ Research Centers
€12M+ Annual Research Funding
500+ Publications/Year
35+ International Partners

Key Research Areas

Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning

Researching advanced AI algorithms and applications, including deep learning, natural language processing, computer vision, and reinforcement learning to solve complex real-world problems.

Cybersecurity & Privacy

Investigating emerging cyber threats and defense mechanisms, developing technologies and methods to protect sensitive data and maintain system integrity.

Big Data & Data Science

Developing efficient tools and methods for processing massive datasets, extracting valuable insights from complex data to support data-driven decision making.

Robotics & Automation

Researching advanced robotic systems, including intelligent robots, autonomous systems, and human-machine interaction to drive industrial automation and smart manufacturing.

Sustainable Technology & Green Computing

Exploring environmentally friendly technologies and energy-efficient computing methods to reduce the IT industry's carbon footprint and promote sustainable development.

Virtual & Augmented Reality

Developing advanced VR/AR technologies and applications, exploring innovative uses in education, healthcare, entertainment, and industrial sectors.
